Shrimp Scampi
Description
This Shrimp Scampi recipe is one of my favourites around the house. It combines a savoury, oily dressing with linguine or fettucine noodles and shrimp that all combine into a mouthwatering dish.
I recommend using raw, uncooked shrimp with this recipe. This recipe serves 2 people.
Ingredients
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 3 tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1lb Shrimp Thawed or Fresh
  • 1/2lb Linguine/Fettucine Pasta
  • 3 cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 1/4 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1/2 cup Dry White Wine
  • Juice of 1 Large Lemon, or 1/3 cup of Lemon Juice
  • Kosher Salt, to taste
  • Black Pepper, to taste
  • Parsley Flakes, to taste
  1. Fill a large pot 2/3 of the way with water and add about 2tsp of salt. Bring to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook until Al Dente.
  3. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat in a large skillet.
  4. Once oil is hot, add butter until fully melted.
  5. Once fully melted, add the garlic and red pepper flakes to the pan, cook until fragrant, approx. 30secs.
  6. Add the wine to the skillet and cook until reduced by half, approx. 3-5mins.
  7. Add the shrimp and cook on each side for approx. 45secs-1min.
  8. Add the lemon juice and stir to incorporate.
  9. Drain the pasta, but reserve some of the water and set aside.
  10. Add the pasta to the skillet and stir to combine. If the sauce looks separated from the noodle, add the reserved pasta water.
  11. Season to taste with salt and pepper, and garnish with parsley flakes.
